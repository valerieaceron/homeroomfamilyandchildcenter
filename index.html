Welcome to Home Room Family and Child Center

We focuses on fostering the holistic growth and development of children and families. We wanted to create an environment where every child and family can experience a home-like learning program supported by services such as Play Based Learning Program, Alternative Applied Behavior Analysis, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, Development Assessment, and Family Counseling.

Our branches are located in Timog, Quezon City, and Muzon, San Jose del Monte, Bulacan.
